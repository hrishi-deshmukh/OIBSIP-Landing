Our Destinations

Nature's Majesty Awaits You

Lago di Braies

Lake Braies is a lake in the Prags Dolomites in South Tyrol, Italy. Picturesque lake in the mountains with climbing opportunities & a nearby historic hotel with dining.

Santorini

Santorini is one of the Cyclades islands in the Aegean Sea. Houses overlook the sea, small islands to the west and beaches made up of black, red and white lava pebbles.

Iceland

Iceland, a Nordic island nation, is defined by its dramatic landscape with volcanoes, geysers, hot springs and lava fields. Its also known for Viking history.

Mount Fuji

Mt. Fuji is an active volcano about 100 kilometers southwest of Tokyo. A pilgrimage site for centuries, it’s considered one of Japan’s 3 sacred mountains.

Tanzania Safari

Tanzania is one of Africa's top safari destinations. The wildlife viewing is out of this world and big cats are especially easy to see.

Mayan Pyramids

Known as the Pyramid of the Magician , it was (according to Maya legend) built by the god of magic, Itzamná, as a training center for priests.

Venice

Venice has no roads, just canals – including the Grand Canal thoroughfare – lined with Renaissance and Gothic palaces.

Grand Canyons

It is one of the most studied landscapes in the world, with extensive fossil records, and rich archeological history.
